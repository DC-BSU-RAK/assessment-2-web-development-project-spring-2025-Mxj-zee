Meet Dr. Jane Smith

Dr. Jane Smith is a licensed clinical psychologist with over 15 years of experience helping individuals lead more fulfilling and emotionally balanced lives. Her expertise includes managing anxiety, depression, trauma, and life transitions.

Credentials

  • Ph.D. in Clinical Psychology – Stanford University
  • Certified Cognitive Behavioral Therapist (CBT)
  • Licensed Mental Health Counselor (LMHC)

Specializations

  • Stress and Anxiety Management
  • Depression and Mood Disorders
  • Grief and Trauma Therapy
  • Mindfulness-Based Cognitive Therapy

Therapeutic Approach

Dr. Smith believes in creating a warm, empathetic, and supportive environment where clients feel safe to explore their emotions and challenges. Her approach is tailored to each client’s unique needs, combining evidence-based practices with compassionate listening.

“My goal is to empower you to discover inner strength and live with more clarity and peace.”
— Dr. Jane Smith
